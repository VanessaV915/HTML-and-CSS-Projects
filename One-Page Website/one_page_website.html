Espresso Drinks


It can be overwhelming when you first start out as a barista with all of the various types of drinks out there

  • Americano

    •       Espresso shots diluted in water

  • Latte

    •       Espresso shots and milk

  • Breve

    •       Espresso shots and half&half

  • Mocha

    •       Espresso shots, milk, and chocolate flavor

  • White Mocha

    •       Espresso shots, milk, and white cholate flavor

  • London Fog

    •       Vanilla syrup, earl grey tea, and steamed milk
