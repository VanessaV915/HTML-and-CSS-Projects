Pitbulls are the Sweetest Pups

There is No Such Thing as a Viscious Breed

Pitbulls have been given a bad image over the years through their activity in dog fighting. The bad rap has nothing to do with the actual dogs, but more to do with the owners of the dogs and their abuse against the breed.

Friends who are owners of sweet and loving pitbulls are the best reference for nature vs. nurture.
